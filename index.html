The YouTube Music
player for macOS menubar.

Faster and Easier Access to YouTube Music from the Menubar.

Download Latest Version
macOS 12+ 9 languages Free download

Petit is an unofficial third-party app. Not affiliated with or endorsed by YouTube or Google.

Everything stays in reach.

  • Menubar Fast

    Access the petit player directly from the menu bar anytime, anywhere, whatever you are doing.

  • Menubar Media

    Enjoy YouTube Music anywhere via our Picture in Picture (PIP).

  • Menubar Media

    Change the playback status directly from the menu bar without having to open the player.

  • Shortcut

    OS X native media key support. Turn the player on/off and change playback status via hot keys in anywhere.

  • Supported Sites

    Currently, only YouTube Music is supported.

  • Languages

    Supports 9 languages: English, Korean, Japanese, Simplified Chinese, Spanish, German, Portuguese, French, and Turkish.

Recent release notes

v0.1.2 2026-04-06 ↓ dmg
  • Fixed an issue where the settings menu appeared behind the app window when clicking the gear icon.
v0.1.1 2026-04-05 ↓ dmg
  • Keyboard shortcuts can now be customized. Go to Settings → Keyboard Shortcuts → Customize Shortcuts...
v0.1.0 2026-04-04 ↓ dmg
  • Completely rewritten from Electron to native Swift/AppKit. Lighter and faster.
  • PiP improved with larger controls, higher quality artwork, and artist name display.
  • Media keys (play/pause, next, previous) are now supported.
  • Shuffle/Repeat icons in menubar now show active/inactive state.
  • Wide Mode added. Expand the player window beyond default width from settings.
  • Keyboard shortcuts changed to Ctrl+Option combos to avoid conflicts with other apps.
  • Added "Import Login from Chrome" in right-click menu for when Google sign-in is blocked in the embedded browser.
  • 9 languages supported: English, Korean, Japanese, Simplified Chinese, Spanish, German, Portuguese, French, and Turkish.
  • Track change notifications removed.
